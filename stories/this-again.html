This Again

Beat: I Burned LA down

(Verse 1)
If we could ever meet again,
would you stop to say hello, and pretend we're still friends
I've been busy filling up the hollow souls buried deep into scars years ago.

Anything you said,
when you walked away
I held it all inside,
until this day

(Chorus)
Ooh, already left the town
to be honest, I don't know how
but I still see you around,
there go many sleepless nights

Going through your words
that you said you cared, but you were never there.
There used to be a fire sparking in your eyes,
and the chemistry that we shared.

(Version 2)
Is it just me,
or all these good times are left behind,
and old faces start to be replaced?

Memories flying around
in flakes of disturbances
I can't help mourning the death of familiarity.

(Chorus)
Ooh, I already left the town
to be honest, I don't know how
but I still see you around,
there go many sleepless nights

Going through your words
that you said you cared, but you were never there.
There used to be a fire sparking in our eyes,
and the chemistry that we shared.

(Chorus)
Ooh, after all these years
Your voice never disappears
I'm just nowhere to be near
forgetting you

I stand here wondering why time passes us by
how many goodbyes I need to feel ok?
