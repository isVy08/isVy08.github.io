Is AI Intelligent Yet?

Is AI intelligent yet?
A question we must not forget
Every paper that comes out
Should speak about it out loud

Do machines get any smarter
Understanding the world the way they should?
Or is it just data hunger?
Only better if they're full

Will AI ever match the brain
that learns so much from so little?
Will AI beware of biases
and correct them as it goes?
Could we teach it about ethics
to also know right from wrong?

But is it right to have one like this
keep getting incredibly strong
One someday can outsmart us
with power going far beyond?
