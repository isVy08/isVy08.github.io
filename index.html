About

This is Vy /vi:/.
I'm a researcher and it happens to be about AI. In this crazy era, there is no limit to what you must study, and any wildest imagination can become the next big thing.

Above all, I am a devoted student of causality. Life to me is nothing but a vast labyrinth of causes and effects, which we yearn to be disentangled from. To reason causally is to understand the essence of intelligence, the universe, and ourselves.

There is a certain wonder in tracing these threads while envisioning a future dominated by intelligent machines. May the wiser creatures prevail.

As a life enthusiast, I do all what it takes to have a life well-lived: write, love, talk, travel, contemplate and eat good food. As a keen writer, I'm engaged in various forms of storytelling: from event scripts, screenplays to musicals, song lyrics and poetry.

Background

I graduated from a Business school and spent around 3 years in Sales, Media Production and Growth Marketing before taking a leap of faith. Now I am a PhD Candidate at Monash University, gratefully funded by CSIRO's Data61, Australia.
