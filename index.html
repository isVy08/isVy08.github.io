About

This is Vy /vi:/.
I'm an AI researcher. My research interests are Causal Inference and Statistical Machine Learning. I am studying artificial causal reasoning while envisioning a future dominated by intelligent machines that can reason and communicate effectively.

As a life enthusiast, I do all what it takes to have a life well-lived: write, love, talk, travel, contemplate and eat good food. I'm a keen writer, engaged in various forms of content writing: from academic papers, event scripts, to screenplays, musicals, song lyrics and poetry.

Background

I had a Bachelor's degree in Business with around 3 years working in Sales, Media Production and Growth Marketing before changing my career. I am currently a PhD Researcher at Monash University, affiliated with CSIRO's Data61, Australia.
