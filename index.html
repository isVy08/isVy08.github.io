About

This is Vy /vi:/.
I am an AI researcher, working at the intersection of Linguistics, AI and Reasoning while envision a future dominated by intelligent machines that can reason and communicate effectively. I am currently exploring Causal ML, that is how to teach machines to reason cause and effects.

I am a life enthusiast. I do all what it takes to have a life well-lived: write, love, talk, travel, contemplate and eat good food. Apart from AI, I also write about what's like to be human and personal struggles which I learn and grow mostly from. Free time to me is a luxury, but when do have it, I watch rom-coms and build tools for learning English.

My Background

My experiences vary. I had a Bachelor's degree in Business with around 3 years working in Sales, Media Production and Growth Marketing before changing my career. Now I am about to graduate from Monash University with a MS in Data Science. A significant portion of my early 20s was spent on learning about human's decision making process, only to wonder whether machines will someday be able to do the same. I pursue research mainly because I am intrigued by problem-solving ideas, and learning makes me feel so good about myself.

What's Else

  • I used to be an assistant manager for a bubble tea shop in Melbourne, where I got to meet and work with people from all walks of life. As we parted ways, I found the self that I lost, and priceless lessons on management, teamwork and relationship.
